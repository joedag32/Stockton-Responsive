You Make the Difference

There are countless examples of Stockton faculty, staff, alumni, and friends going well above and beyond the norm to ensure that students complete their degrees. Extra help in the classroom, rides home on break, care and concern in difficult times; these expressions are not exclusive to Stockton, but they take on a deep meaning here. We invite you to learn more at www.stockton.edu/foundation.

Distinctive Stockton Students

Students at The Richard Stockton College of New Jersey are scholars, leaders, world travelers, researchers and innovators. “Distinctive Stockton Students” is a blog that shows off the successes of our undergraduate and graduate students and new alumni.

Living on Campus

Many of our students reside on-campus in two distinct residential areas--the apartment complexes and the residence halls--each with its own distinct lifestyle. The College features Living Learning Communities, designed to connect students, faculty and staff around curricular and co-curricular themes and interests. These communities support and enhance student success in academic, personal and social development.
